Lasanga

Lasanga is a delicious dish that is made with layers of pasta, meat, and cheese. It is a great dish to make for a large group of people.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup parsley
  • 1/2 cup basil
  • 1/2 cup oregano
  • 1/2 cup salt
  • 1/2 cup pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Chop onion and garlic.
  3. Combine ground beef and pork in a large skillet.
  4. Stir in onion and garlic.
  5. Cook until meat is browned.
  6. Drain grease.
  7. Stir in tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, water, and red wine.
  8. Bring to a boil.
  9. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes.
  10. Stir in parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, ricotta cheese, parsley, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  11. Spread 1/3 of the meat mixture in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  12. Top with 3 uncooked lasagna noodles.
  13. Spread 1/3 of the meat mixture over the noodles.
  14. Repeat layers twice.
  15. Bake for 1 hour in the preheated oven.
