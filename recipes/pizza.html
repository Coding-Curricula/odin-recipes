Pizza

Pizza is a delicious dish that is made with layers of dough, sauce, and cheese. It is a great dish to make for a large group of people.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza crust
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup green peppers
  • 1/2 cup black olives

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Spread pizza sauce on pizza crust.
  3. Top with mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, mushrooms, green peppers, and black olives.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
