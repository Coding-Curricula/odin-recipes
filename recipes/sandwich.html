Sandwich

A sandwich is a delicious dish that is made with layers of bread, sauce, and cheese. It is a great dish to make for a large group of people.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1/2 cup turkey
  • 1/2 cup cheese

Directions

  1. Spread turkey on one slice of bread.
  2. Spread cheese on the other slice of bread.
  3. Place the two slices of bread together.
  4. Enjoy!
