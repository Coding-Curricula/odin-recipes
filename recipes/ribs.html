Ribs

Ribs are a delicious dish that is made with layers of meat and sauce. It is a great dish to make for a large group of people.

Ingredients

  • rack of bone in baby back ribs
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup onion powder
  • 1/2 cup salt
  • 1/2 cup pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Combine brown sugar, honey, soy sauce, ketchup, apple cider vinegar, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
  3. Place ribs in a large roasting pan.
  4. Pour sauce over ribs.
  5. Roast for 2 hours.
  6. Remove from oven and let cool for 10 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
